About Us

Senthil Scans, the popular Radiodiagnostic centre in Thanjavur started in 2012, is now a full-fledged, advanced centre. The centre is equipped with all state of art machines for a diagnostic centre including Ultrasound, CT scan, MRI scan, Mammogram, XRay, Laboratory, etc.,

All Radiodiagnostic investigations and lab facilities under one roof!

We are ISO and CEA certified and would like to improve our standards to the next level.

Quality patient service is our motto and we try our best everyday to give good patient care.

NABH Accreditation first scan centre in delta district.

National Accreditation Board for Hospital & Healthcare Providers (NABH) is a constituent board of quality council of India, set up establish and operate accreditation programme for health care organizations.

The standard provide framework for quality care of patients and necessary improvements in hospitals by systemizing the hospital operations and protocols.
